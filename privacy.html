Privacy

Scope

This page describes how FiveTwoOh, Inc. handles information collected through this company website. It applies only to this website. The SilentMatch mobile application has its own separate privacy policy, which will be made available within the app and at the time the app is distributed.

Information We Collect on This Website

This website is a static informational site. It does not require you to create an account, and it does not collect personal information from visitors who simply view the pages.

If you contact us by email at the address listed on this site, we receive the email content you send, along with your email address and any information you choose to include. We use that information only to respond to your inquiry.

How We Use Contact Information

Email addresses and message content sent to us are used solely to respond to inquiries. We do not sell or rent contact information to third parties.

Cookies and Tracking

This website does not set tracking cookies and does not use third-party analytics on its pages.

Third-Party Hosting

This website is hosted by a third-party hosting provider. The hosting provider may automatically record standard server logs (such as IP address and timestamp) as part of normal operation. These logs are not used by FiveTwoOh, Inc. to identify individual visitors.

SilentMatch Application

SilentMatch, the mobile application produced by FiveTwoOh, Inc., is a separate product and has its own privacy policy. That policy will govern any information collected through the app itself, and it will be presented to users within the app and through the App Store listing at the time of distribution.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy statement, contact stu@fivetwooh.com.
